# About Workflow Docs

Workflow Docs is a project of [Lemma Legal Consulting](https://lemmalegal.com), a firm dedicated to bridging the gap between law and technology.

## Deep Roots in Legal Aid

Our team isn't just building software; we are attorneys and developers with decades of combined experience on the front lines of legal aid. We understand the unique challenges of public interest law because we've lived them.

- **Quinten Steenhuis (Founder)**: A computer scientist and attorney with **12 years of experience as a legal aid litigator**. He is a nationally recognized expert in legal automation, a 2021 ABA Legal Rebel, and the author of the White House-recognized eviction defense tool, *MADE*.
- **Toby Fey**: Spent **15 years as a legal aid housing lawyer**, helping thousands of tenants before transitioning into expert document automation.
- **Rami Lorca**: Formerly a **legal aid attorney in Boston**, where he first pioneered the use of automation technologies to expand access to justice.

## Our Mission

We specialize in building smarter, deeply integrated tools for **LegalServer** users. Our mission is to help legal aid organizations and law firms streamline their document workflows—from initial intake to final signature—so they can focus on what matters most: serving their clients.

By combining legal expertise with modern software engineering, we ensure that every template and automation we build is legally sound, user-friendly, and technically robust.
