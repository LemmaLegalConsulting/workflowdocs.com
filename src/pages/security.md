# Security & Data Privacy

Your clients' data is protected at every step.

## Bank-level security
Enterprise-grade encryption and security protocols protect all data in transit and at rest.

## Data stays in LegalServer
LegalServer stays the permanent system of record.

## AI enterprise privacy
AI features use OpenAI's enterprise privacy policy. Your data is never stored or used for training purposes.

## Ethical AI design
AI features ask questions and gather information, but never give clients legal advice or recommendations.
