# Security & Data Privacy

Your clients' data is protected at every step. We prioritize confidentiality and adhere to strict enterprise standards.

## Data Stays in LegalServer
LegalServer remains the permanent system of record. Workflow Docs acts as a bridge to help you gather information and generate documents, but the source of truth never changes.

## Encryption & Retention
All data processed by Workflow Docs is **encrypted in transit and at rest**. We only retain data for as long as is strictly necessary to complete the document assembly process. For more details on our data handling, please see the [Lemma Legal Privacy Policy](https://lemmalegal.com/privacy).

## Microsoft Azure AI Enterprise Privacy
Our AI features are powered by **Microsoft Azure OpenAI Service**. Unlike consumer AI tools, our integration uses Microsoft's **Enterprise Privacy Policy**, which means:
- Your data is **never used to train** Microsoft's underlying AI models.
- Data entered into the system is isolated to your organization's environment.
- Microsoft adheres to HIPAA, SOC 2, and other rigorous compliance standards.

## Ethical AI Design
AI features are designed to be "human-in-the-loop." They help ask questions and gather information based on your templates, but they **never provide legal advice**, make recommendations, or replace an advocate's judgment.
